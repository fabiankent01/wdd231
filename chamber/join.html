Membership Tiers & Benefits

NP Membership (Non-Profit)

Cost: Free
  • Access to networking events with other non-profit organizations.
  • Free or discounted participation in workshops and training sessions.
  • Inclusion in the organization's directory for increased visibility.

Bronze Membership

Cost: $250 per year
  • Basic advertising opportunities on the organization's website or newsletter.
  • 10% discount on event registrations.
  • Quarterly members-only webinar on industry trends and best practices.

Silver Membership

Cost: $500 per year
  • Enhanced advertising exposure on the organization's website, newsletter, and social media.
  • 15% discount on event registrations.
  • Monthly members-only webinars covering specific topics in-depth.
  • Priority access to limited-capacity events.

Gold Membership

Cost: $1000 per year
  • Premium advertising slots on the organization's website, newsletter, and social media.
  • 20% discount on event registrations.
  • Access to an annual weekend retreat or conference at a discounted rate.
  • Priority access to limited-capacity events.
  • Priority access to all events, workshops, and training sessions.
